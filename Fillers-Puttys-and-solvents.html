Fillers

Body filler is an important product in automotive painting, and we at Tonys Colours are proud to offer the best products at great prices.


Our body filler is perfect for spray painters and panel beaters, and our expert advice will ensure that you get the perfect finish every time. So don't hesitate to contact us today and see how we can help you achieve the perfect paint job.

Putty's

We have a wide selection of automotive paint ancillaries, including Stone chip, Rubberbak, Spot putty, Spray fillers and more.

Stone Chips

Stone Chip is a premium acrylic coating used to protect vehicles and other equipment against corrosion, heat, salt water spray, petrol, oil and some solvents.


It also provides excellent resistance to impact, is extremely hard wearing and easy to clean.
