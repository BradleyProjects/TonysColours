State of the Art Training Center Completed.

  • Button
  • Button
  • Button
  • Button

We are proud to announce that our long awaited state of the art Training Center has been completed and has already put many learners through their paces. This magnificent facility will provide the platform for Tony’s Colours to empower and strengthen the skill set of tradesmen within our industry as well as the economy at large. It will also further enhance our already wonderful relationship we share with our customers.


The Center contains a fully equipped Mixing Room, Spray Booth, Preparation Bay and Classroom. The Center has uniquely fused technology into the process actually creating a more personal and focused atmosphere for learners to obtain optimal results and a non invasive quality experience. Each step of the process utilises fully fledged interactive cameras and speakers to compliment and magnify the ability of the learner to take in the information.
