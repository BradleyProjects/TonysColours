Commercial Paint

2K Paint

2K paint is a two-component acrylic paint for cars and industrial purposes. 2k clear coat is composed of a special type of additive to obtain superior hardness and gloss. Most affordable 2k spray paint & 2k paint prices on the market.

Clearcoat

The vehicle clear coat is the final coat of paint applied to your car, going over the basecoat paint. It's a transparent, glossy coating that is meant to enhance the look of your paint and provide protection from the environment and UV rays from the sun. 


Enquire now Call us now

Hardener

The primary ingredient in paint hardener is sodium polyacrylate, which is a crystallized salt product. The salt crystals absorb moisture very quickly and turn the paint into a solid, rubbery substance. So effectively drying your paint much faster!

Enquire now Call us now

Primers

In the automotive world, the term primer paint generally refers to a paint-like substance that's usually applied to the freshly sanded metal before the topcoat. Much like paint, different types of automotive primers can be applied with a spray gun and are allowed to dry thoroughly between coats. This metal primer spray paint is to protect the metal from rusting. We offer the the best primer paint price on the market.

QD Paints

Quick-drying durable paint suitable for the protection and decoration of all interior and exterior steel surfaces such as farm and garden implements, steel furniture and machinery.

N/C Paint

NC Paint finish is that long-wearing, attractive, high-gloss finish that is mostly seen on interior wooden surfaces and old cars.


Enquire now Call us now

PMA - Water Based

This water based paint & water based spray paint is resistant to yellowing and provides excellent resistance to environmental conditions and weathering as well as a very good scratch resistance. The gloss level is 10 - 15 gloss units at an angle of 60°, although this value can vary greatly due to structure and painting process.

PMI - Solvent Based

This product is resistant to yellowing and provides excellent resistance to environmental conditions and weathering as well as a very good scratch resistance. The gloss level is 10 - 90 gloss units at an angle of 60°, although this value can vary greatly due to structure and painting process.
