Spray Guns

There are many benefits to using a spray gun when painting. They can help you achieve a smooth, even finish with less effort than painting with a brush or roller. Spray guns also allow you to cover large areas quickly and easily.


When choosing a spray gun, it is important to consider the type of paint you will be using, as well as the size of the area you need to cover. There are many different types of spray guns on the market, so it is important to do your research before making a purchase.


We stock a wide range of spray guns, suitable for both professional, commercial or DIY use. We also offer expert advice to help you choose the right gun for your needs. Visit us today to find the perfect spray gun for your next painting project.

Infrared heaters

As the weather gets colder and inconsistent, many people are looking for ways to improve their heating situation in order to dry paint faster. One option that is gaining popularity is the use of infrared heaters.


These heaters work by emitting infrared radiation, which is then absorbed by objects in the room. This radiation heats up the objects, which in turn heats up the air around them and decreases drying time in the process.﻿

Orbital Sanders

Orbital sanders are hand-held tools that work by rotating themselves elliptically to create a smooth surface. You can even use them to remove rust or paint. If you need a smooth surface in a short time, an orbital sander might be your best bet.
